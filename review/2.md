                <p>$V1 = ?$ (volume of concentrate needed)</p>
                [cite_start]<p>$C2 = \frac{1}{200} = 0.005$ (desired final concentration) [cite: 57]</p>
                [cite_start]<p>$V2 = 40~ml$ (desired final volume) [cite: 57]</p>
                [cite_start]<p>$0.01 \times V1 = 0.005 \times 40$ [cite: 58]</p>
                [cite_start]<p>$V1 = \frac{0.005 \times 40}{0.01} = \frac{0.2}{0.01}$ [cite: 58]</p>
