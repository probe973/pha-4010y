Body Mass Index

The formula for body mass index (BMI):

$BMI=\frac{\text{Mass}(\text{kg})}{(\text{Height}(\text{m}))^{2}}$

Body Surface Area

The formula for body surface area (BSA):

$BSA(\text{m}^{2})=\sqrt{\frac{\text{Height}(\text{cm})\times \text{Mass}(\text{Kg})}{3600}}$

The following information may be useful:

$\sqrt{2}\approx1.41\quad\sqrt{3}\approx1.73\quad\sqrt{5}\approx2.24\quad\sqrt{6}\approx2.45$

  1. Find the BSA of the following people:

    a. Height=180 $\text{cm}$, Mass=60 $\text{kg}$

    b. Height=144 $\text{cm}$, Mass=50 $\text{kg}$

    c. Height=1.92 $\text{m}$, Mass=112.5 $\text{kg}$



Ideal Body Weight (IBW)

Use this formula for IBW:

Female: IBW ($\text{kg}$) = Height ($\text{cm}$)$\times0.9-92$

Male: IBW ($\text{kg}$) = Height ($\text{cm}$)$\times0.9-88$

  1. What is the IBW for the following:

    a. Male, Height = 1.7 m
