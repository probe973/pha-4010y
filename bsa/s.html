  1. What is the body surface area of someone who is 1.8 metres tall and 64.8 Kg in weight?
    Convert height to cm: 1.8 metres = 180 cm. BSA = √((180 cm × 64.8 Kg) ÷ 3600) Simplify the fraction: (180 × 64.8) ÷ 3600 Cancel a zero from the numerator (180) and denominator (3600) by dividing both by 10: = (18 × 64.8) ÷ 360 Divide the numerator (18) and denominator (360) by 18: = (1 × 64.8) ÷ 20 Now, divide 64.8 by 20: = 3.24 BSA = √3.24 From the given list, √3.24 = 1.8.
    Solution: 1.8 m²
