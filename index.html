Further Topic Based Practice

Below you will find links to questions on individual topics.

Unit Conversions
Converting between different units of mass, volume, and length.
Concentration Calculations
Expressing concentrations in various forms (mg/mL, %w/v, 1 in X) and converting between them.
Dosage and Volume Calculations
Determining the amount of drug or volume of solution required based on given concentrations and desired doses.
Molarity and Mole Calculations
Calculating quantities of substances based on molecular weights and molarity, often involving millimoles.
Dilution Principles
Applying the C1V1 = C2V2 formula to calculate required volumes for dilutions.
Patient Specific Conversions
Converting patient height and weight between imperial and metric units, and temperature conversions.
Body Surface Area (BSA) Calculation
Using a specific formula to determine Body Surface Area from height and weight.
Drug Administration Rates
Calculating the total amount of drug administered over time based on a given rate.
Formula Scaling
Calculating ingredient quantities for larger batches of multi-component preparations based on a standard formula.
